Welcome to Crowd Control.

Crowd Control lets you take control of a 3D rendering of St Andrews' only nightclub, Club 601. We're live on Facebook and Teams right now, where your designs will be broadcast to the world.

Join the queue and have a play with the controls in Rehearsal Mode while you're waiting, to get a feel for how it works. Once it's your turn, you'll be switched into Live Mode and your controls will activate, so you can start making your designs. The bar at the bottom of the screen will let you know what mode you're in.

Rehearsal mode - your controls aren't active so your designs won't appear live, but you can practise.
